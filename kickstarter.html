Leave Me Alone Sweater Kickstarter -- Launches March 28

Made in the USA out of high quality hemp and recycled fiber fleece, this is a cozy cowl neck sweater that can be zipped over the head in Leave Me Alone mode.

Whether you're being badgered by your family or coworkers, with someone who just can't take a hint, or trying to sleep in the airport, the Leave Me Alone Sweater has you covered. Invented by Ruth Grace Wong, the Leave Me Alone Sweater went viral, and now we need to put together an order of 300 sweaters to be able to have them manufactured.

Features

Made from a hemp fleece that is 55% hemp and 45% recycled plastic, the Leave Me Alone Sweater features a charcoal color, thumbholes, pockets, cowl neck, and zipper.
